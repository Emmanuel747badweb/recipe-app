Fried rice

Fried rice is a delcious Nigerian dish made by cooking Rice with Chicken stock, stir-fried vegetables, and protein like chicken.It is colorful, tasty and often served as parties or family meals.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Onions
  • Curry powder
  • Seasonings(maggi)
  • Salt
  • Chicken stock
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Paboil rice and set aside
  2. Cook rice n chicken stock and curry,maggi and salt
  3. Stir-fry onions, vegetables and diced chickens
  4. Mix rice with stir-fry until well combined
  5. Serve hot and enjoy
